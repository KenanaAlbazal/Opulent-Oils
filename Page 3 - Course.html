Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist looking to refine your skills, our school offers a nurturing and inspiring environment for all levels.

Introduction to Oil Painting

This beginner-level course is designed for those who are new to oil painting. You'll learn the basics of oil painting, including materials, techniques, and foundational skills. By the end of this course, you will have completed several projects and gained the confidence to continue exploring oil painting on your own.

Intermediate Oil Painting

Building on basic skills, this intermediate course delves deeper into oil painting techniques and explores different artistic styles. Ideal for students who have some experience with oil painting, this course will help you refine your skills and develop your own artistic voice.

Masterclass in Oil Painting

This advanced course is designed for experienced artists who wish to work on personal projects under the guidance of a professional instructor. Focused on individual development, this course provides the opportunity to work on larger or more complex pieces and receive personalized feedback and mentorship.
About Us
